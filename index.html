About Me

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA), where I previously graduated with a Licenciatura in Data Science (B.S. + M.Sc. equivalent) with a GPA of 8.9/10.

My research focuses on Explainable AI (XAI) for multimodal models. My thesis explored "Similarity between word representations generated by audio and text models", studying convergence towards universal representations across modalities using metrics like Linear CKA and ASIF with modern architectures (BEATs, EncodecMAE, BERT).

I have published at top-tier venues including AAAI, ACL, and Interspeech, and I'm a recipient of the CONICET Doctoral Fellowship and the IEEE SPS SigMA mentorship award.
